Coworking Night
Learn. Collaborate. Get Sh*t Done.

Every Wednesday - 6-11pm - AL.com office - 200 Westside Sq, Huntsville

Coworking Night is a free community events platform for creative professionals.

This week @ Coworking Night
Find out what's happening this week

 

Learn.

Each week begins with a headliner presentation from Huntsville's brightest technology leaders and entrepreneurs.

 

Collaborate.

Recurring workshops and classes covering a variety of subjects are held each week. And local nonprofits meet with members to bring positive change to the Huntsville community.

 

Get Sh*t Done.

Working on a personal project? It can be hard to find time (or peace and quiet) to make any progress. GSD time is the perfect opportunity to do just that. Collaboration is welcome, but distractions are minimal.
